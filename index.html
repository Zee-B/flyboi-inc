About the Label

Born Free.
Built to Last.

Flyboy I.N.C was born in November 2017 from an act of artistic courage. When Kizz Daniel — one of Nigeria's most gifted voices — walked away from G-Worldwide Entertainment, he didn't just leave a label. He built his own.

Operating from Lagos, Flyboy I.N.C is an independent record label, management company, and creative force committed to developing world-class African talent. From Afropop to Afrobeats, the label has helped define the sound of a generation — reaching hundreds of millions of listeners across every continent.
